About SurplusShare

Our mission, vision, and the story behind the community.

Our Mission

At SurplusShare, we're dedicated to creating a world where no edible food goes to waste. We believe in the power of community connections to solve local problems while building stronger neighborhoods.

Founded in 2023, SurplusShare started as a simple idea: what if we could create a platform that makes it easy for neighbors to share excess food instead of throwing it away?

Today, we're a growing community of food-sharers committed to reducing waste, fighting food insecurity, and creating more resilient local food systems.

The Problem We're Solving

Food waste is a massive global problem:

  • Approximately 1/3 of all food produced globally is wasted
  • In the USA alone, 30-40% of the food supply becomes waste
  • The average family throws away $1,500 worth of food annually
  • Meanwhile, millions of people experience food insecurity

By connecting those with excess food to those who can use it, we're creating a simple solution that benefits everyone involved and our planet.
