Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share food on SurplusShare?

Creating a listing is simple! Just click on "Share Food" from the homepage, upload a photo of your surplus food, provide a brief description, and set your location and availability.

Is it safe to share and receive food?

We recommend following basic food safety guidelines. Only share food that is still fresh and has been stored properly. As a recipient, use your judgment and ask questions about the food's preparation and storage.

Is SurplusShare available in my area?

SurplusShare is community-driven and can work anywhere people want to share food! Check the app to see if there are active users in your area, and if not, be the first to start sharing!

How can I suggest features or report issues?

We welcome your feedback! Use the contact form on this page or email us directly at feedback@surplusshare.org with your suggestions or issues.
