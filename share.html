Sharing Guidelines

Food Safety

  • Only share food that is still fresh and safe to eat
  • Provide accurate information about when it was prepared
  • Mention any allergens that may be present
  • Package food properly for safe handling

Good Photos

  • Take clear, well-lit photos of your food items
  • Show the actual items you're sharing, not stock photos
  • Include packaging if relevant
  • Photos help recipients know what to expect

Communication

  • Be responsive to messages from interested recipients
  • Provide clear pickup instructions
  • Update or remove your listing when the food is no longer available
